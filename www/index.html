DATA PRIVACY DISCLOSURE

To automate your daily transaction ledger and protect your business from fraud, Ngabo requests permission to read transaction notifications on this device.

BY TAPPING "I ACCEPT & ACTIVATE":

  1. You grant Ngabo explicit authorization to parse native transaction notifications sent by mobile money networks (MTN Uganda, Airtel Uganda) and banking agents.
  2. Your business records are stored securely ONLY on this physical phone's encrypted memory. We DO NOT send your transaction data to external servers without your direct permission.
  3. You retain the right to erase all captured data instantly at any time via the "Wipe Data & Reset" tool in your settings panel.
  4. Data capture is performed entirely locally and offline, minimizing risk and protecting your financial privacy.

Compliant with Uganda's Data Protection and Privacy Act 2019 (NITA-U / PDPO)
